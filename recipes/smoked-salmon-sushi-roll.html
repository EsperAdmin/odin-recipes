Smoked Salmon Sushi Roll

Description

Homemade salmon roll made with sushi rice, nori, and smoked salmon. This is a very basic and easy recipe for making sushi. It might take a while to learn how to make a perfect roll, but you'll have fun trying! Add wasabi (Japanese horseradish) for a good kick!

Ingredients

  • sushi rice
  • rice wine vinegar
  • seaweed
  • wasabi paste
  • smoked salmon
  • cucumber
  • avocado

Steps

  1. Soak and cook the rice
  2. mix in the vinegar
  3. cool in an even layer on a plate
  4. Assemble the sushi rolls
  5. Roll the sushi tightly and cut into eight pieces
